About ClarityCare Health Network

Mission-driven care, built for the way people live today.

Mission, vision & values

Access

We believe everyone deserves timely, affordable care. Same-day options, telehealth, and a patient portal remove barriers so you can get care when and where you need it.

Compassion

Care should feel human. We listen, explain, and partner with you—treating the whole person, not just the condition.

Safety

Your health information is protected. We follow best practices for data security, privacy, and clinical quality.

Innovation

We use technology to improve care—not replace it. Digital tools support our clinicians and make your experience smoother.

Our story

ClarityCare Health Network was founded to bring together compassionate care and modern technology. We saw that patients and providers alike were frustrated by fragmented systems, long wait times, and outdated workflows. We built a tech-forward model from the ground up—with a patient portal, telehealth, and coordinated care across clinics—so that technology serves people, not the other way around.

We collaborate with a long-term IT partner to keep our systems secure and available. That partnership allows us to focus on what we do best: care.
