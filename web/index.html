About Us

Founded: 1984 · Type: Privately held small business, 11–50 employees.

Leadership: Michael Alan Prestwood — technologist, software architect, and author (e.g., "30 Philosophers").

Started as Prestwood Software focused on desktop database apps (Paradox, Delphi, Visual Basic), then expanded into web development, .NET, and the PSC ASP framework before rebranding to Prestwood IT Solutions as a full MSP and consulting firm.

Today: Managed IT, Microsoft 365, AI integration, and WordPress/web solutions with an emphasis on long-term relationships and process-driven delivery (Prestwood Software Development Process / PSDP).

Reputation: Member of Roseville and Citrus Heights chambers; repeatedly voted "Best Computer Repair/IT Services" and "Best Web Design and Service Company" in Citrus Heights.

Staff: Systems engineers with computer-science degrees or nearing completion, holding certifications like CompTIA A+/Network+/Security+, Microsoft, Cisco, AWS, Google, and compliance credentials.
