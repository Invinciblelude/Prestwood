Patient Portal

Your health information, secure and accessible—anytime, anywhere.

Key features

Manage your care from one secure place.

View test results and visit summaries

See lab results, imaging reports, and visit notes as soon as they’re available. No more waiting for a phone call.

Secure messaging with your care team

Ask questions, request refills, or share updates with your providers through encrypted messaging—with responses typically within one business day.

Online bill pay

View statements and pay your balance securely. Set up payment plans or update insurance information.

Appointment scheduling and telehealth links

Book appointments, reschedule, or join virtual visits with a single click. All links and reminders are sent securely.

We use data encryption, strong access controls, and industry best practices to protect your information. Your health data is never shared outside your care team without your consent.

Get started in 3 steps

1

Receive your invite

After your first visit, we’ll send you an email invitation to create your portal account. Check your inbox (and spam folder).

2

Create your secure login

Follow the link to set up your username and password. Use a strong, unique password and keep it private.

3

Manage your care

Log in anytime to view results, message your care team, schedule appointments, and pay bills.
