Frequently asked questions

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans. When you book an appointment, our team will verify your coverage. You can also call us to confirm before your visit.

Can I get a same-day appointment?

Yes. We offer same-day in-person and virtual visits when availability allows. Book online or call to check same-day options.

How does telehealth work?

After you schedule a virtual visit, you’ll receive an email with a secure link. At your appointment time, click the link to join the video visit from your phone, tablet, or computer. No special software is required.

Is my health information safe online?

Yes. We use encryption, strong access controls, and industry best practices to protect your data. Our systems are monitored and backed up by a dedicated IT partner. Your information is never shared outside your care team without your consent.

What do I need for a telehealth visit?

A device with a camera and microphone (phone, tablet, or computer), a stable internet connection, and a private, quiet space. You’ll receive a link before your appointment—no app download required.
